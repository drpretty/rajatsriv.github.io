About Batman

Batman, also known as Bruce Wayne, is a superhero who defends Gotham City. With no superpowers, his unmatched intellect, detective skills, and cutting-edge technology enable him to fight crime.

Driven by tragedy and a relentless pursuit of justice, Batman has become a symbol of hope for Gotham's citizens. Explore his journey, his arsenal of gadgets, and the dark lore behind the man behind the mask.
